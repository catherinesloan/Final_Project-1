About

On this website, you will be able to predict house prices of properties listed for sale in Melbourne. This will allow potential property buyers to assess whether or not current house listings are over or undervalued and whether or not to bid on a property.

The price predictor also takes into account Victorian crime rates which will provide further insight in whether crime rates affect house prices.

As opposed to other online price predictors, you will not be required to pay any fees or disclose personal details.
